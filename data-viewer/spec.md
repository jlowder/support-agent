# data viewer specification

1. display is hierarchical in nature. Customers are shown at the top level, with their orders listed below in collapsed form. When expanded, orders will reveal the items ordered and their quantities.

2. This tool only displays data available in the local_crm.json file, and does not allow editing or deletion of records, or initiation of refunds.

3. the file "local_crm.json" is processed dynamically to produce the HTML output.
